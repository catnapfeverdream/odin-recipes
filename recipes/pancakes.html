Fluffy Vegan Pancakes

Home

Description

Who doesn't love pancakes?! Whether you want to smother them in syrup, chocolate chips, and whipped cream or dribble some fresh berries on top - pancakes are the answer to all of your problems!

Full credit to Nora Cooks, one of my favorite vegan cooking sites that I learned this fantastic fluffy pancake recipe from.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp canola oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Pour the milk, water, and oil into the bowl with the dry ingredients and stir with a large spoon until just combined. A few lumps are okay; do not overmix or your pancakes won't be as fluffy!
  3. Heat a large griddle or pan over medium-high heat. Grease the pan with vegan butter or coconut oil, and drop about 1/3 cup of the batter onto it. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown on the other side, about 1-2 minutes. Repeat with all of the remaining batter.
  4. Serve with toppings of choice: vegan butter, chocolate chips, pure maple syrup, fresh fruit and berries, whipped cream, mac and cheese... the pancake is your oyster!
