Coconut Lime Curry

Home

Description

Going back to an old favorite of mine here. And I definitely shouldn't be created a recipes database while I'm hungry because my mouth is watering and my stomach is growling...

This recipe creates a beautiful, aromatic curry sauce that is best served with homemade naan, some fluffy rice, and a protein of choice - my personal favorite is tofu as this recipe calls for, but since it's ultra-processed, my healthy brain says cauliflower is a good alternative.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 onion, sliced/diced
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • knob of ginger, grated
  • 1-2 tbsp red curry paste
  • 1-2 tbsp sriracha/hot sauce
  • 250-300 ml coconut milk
  • 1-2 tsp brown sugar
  • 1-2 tbsp light soy sauce
  • juice of 1 lemon or lime

Steps

  1. Gently heat 1-2 tbsp of sesame oil in a pan and fry firm tofu slices until golden brown on both sides. Optional: coat the tofu slices lightly in corn flour for a more crisp and golden texture.
  2. Remove the tofu and set aside. In the same pan, stir in 1 tbsp sesame oil, sliced/diced onion, minced garlic, grated ginger, red curry paste, and the hot sauce.
  3. Stir it all together and, once fragrant, add the coconut milk and then stir in: brown sugar, light soy sauce, and the juice of the lemon or lime - dealer's choice!
  4. Once simmering, add back the cooked tofu and stir it all around for good measure. Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds, serve over rice, and eat your heart out.
