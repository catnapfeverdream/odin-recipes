Loaded Potato Soup

Home

Description

This recipe is definitely what I'm making for dinner tonight. It's so easy, even you can make it! Full credit to the @veganer.wandel instagram account that I learned it from.

With minimal ingredients and cooktime, this recipe is essentially a potato soup for beginners. Kind of like how this Odin Project is an HTML practice for beginners.

Ingredients

Soup:

  • 400 g potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp vegan butter
  • 250 ml vegan cream
  • 250 ml oat milk
  • 400 ml vegetable stock
  • 1 handful of vegan cheese
  • 1 tsp salt, pepper, nutmeg, and paprika

Tofu Bacon:

  • 400 g tofu
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp agave syrup, honey, or maple syrup

Toppings:

  • chopped spring onions
  • chili flakes
  • maybe some more cheese

Steps

  1. Dice the tofu and saute. Season with agave syrup and smoked paprika and then set aside.
  2. Saute the onions and the celery.
  3. Add the flour, butter, soy milk and cream and bring everything to a boil while stirring.
  4. Add the potatoes and vegetable stock and simmer for 30ish minutes.
  5. Stir in the shredded cheese, season, and add toppings.
  6. Don't forget to make some bread for dipping and you're ready to rock!
